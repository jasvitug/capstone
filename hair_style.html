Different Hair Styles For Women

*Dutch Flower Braid Hairstyle*


You cannot avoid those admiring glances if you are wearing the Dutch flower braid hairstyle. It is essentially a Dutch braid put together in the shape of a flower. This elaborate and detailed hairstyle is ideal for weddings and other occasions.




*Flat Waves Hairstyle*



The Flat Wave Hairstyle is the new runway favorite! This minimal yet oh-so-chic hairstyle involves light curls from the mid-length to the tip of your hair, while the top of your hair remains straight and sleek.




*Chic Flower Petal Updo Hairstyle*



The bridesmaids too need to choose a hairstyle for the memorable evening. Try the Floral Petal Updo Hairstyle that is perfect for the occasion.




*Bow Bun Hairstyle*



Going out for a music festival and want to look different? Say cool and quirky with a bow bun hairstyle. This easy to create hairdo is a great choice if you are heading out in a jiffy.




*Waterfall Braid Hairstyle*



Waterfall Braid is a chic hairstyle that will make your loose hair look mesmerizing. A waterfall braid looks less like a plait and more like a hair accessory. You may also style the rest of your hair into another braid and create a unique look.




*Loop Waterfall Braid Hairstyle*



Go for the Loop Waterfall Braid Hairstyle if you want to keep it simple and let your tresses flow loose. If you are short on time, this hairstyle is a great way to look like a fashionista.



Different Hair Styles For Men

*Quiff with Tapered Sides and Beard*


Here we have a super stylish quiff with short tapered sides and not a fade. While quiff hairstyles can be styled into almost any hair type, it’s best if you have thick, straight hair.




*Pompadour with Mid Fade and Beard*



The modern pompadour is a hot variation of the retro style popularized by Rock and Roll in the 50’s and 60’s. The hairstyle continues to be widespread and requires a good pomade when styling.




*Hard Part Comb Over*



The hard comb over is a thicker and more voluminous variation of the side part. If you have thick, straight hair and want longer hair on top, this hairstyle is a great option.




*Long Slicked Back Hair*



Slicked back hair always looks great with a disconnected undercut. If you’re thinking about a textured hairstyle on top, consider a short cut on the sides to really accentuate the style.
